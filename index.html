Let's know your Birthday is Palindrome or not?

Do You know what is Palindrome?

Palindrome is a word or number which reads same backwards as forward.

Let's Do It.

Enter your Birthday and we will tell you if it is a Palindrome.

This app checks your birthdate in 6 formats dd-mm-yyyy,mm-dd-yyyy ,yyyy-dd-mm,yyyy-mm-dd,dd-mm-yy, mm-dd-yy e.g. if your birthdate is 01 Aug 1995, then app will check for 01081995, 08011995, 19950108, 19950801, 010895,080195.
